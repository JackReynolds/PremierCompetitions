How It Works

Welcome to Premier Competitions! We hope you enjoy your time browsing our amazing prizes up for grabs.

Here's how to get started:

  • Find a competition you want to enter
  • Click "More Info"
  • Select how many numbers you would like to take
  • Answer our skill based qualifying question
  • Complete your payment
  • Provided you have answered the question correctly, you will receive an email with confirmation of your payment and ticket number
